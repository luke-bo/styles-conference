Speakers

We’re happy to welcome over twenty speakers to present on the industries latest technologies. Prepare for an inspiration extravaganza.

Shay Howe

Less Is More: How Constraints Cultivate Growth

By settling constraints, we force ourselves to be more productive. They help us make decisions, creating focus around the problem we are trying to solve. They improve our consistency, which provides a better experience for our users. And they help us grow, a valuable asset in times of innovation.

About Shay

As a designer and front end developer, Shay Howe has a passion for solving problems while building creative and intuitive products. Shay specializes in product design and interface development, specialties in which he regularly writes and speaks about.
